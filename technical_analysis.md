## Dataset Overview  
- **Repository**: Jabulisilec/demand-forecasting  
- **Total Records**: 10000  
- **Features**:  
  - `date`: Date of the record  
  - `product_id`: Unique identifier for each product  
  - `demand`: Demand value for the corresponding product on the specified date
